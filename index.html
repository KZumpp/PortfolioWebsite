About Me

Hey! I'm Katie, and I'm a Full stack Java Developer with a passion for Front End design, creative problem solving, and buckets of coffee. Resident of the PNW, mom to a small dog with a big attitude, and enthusiastic about tech!

More About Me

My coding journey started in fall of 2021, when I quit a dead-end office job and was consumed by the burn out of doing stuff that paid my bills but did nothing to inspire me in any way. I decided to sit down and teach myself the basics of Python since I've always been great with technology. It was about halfway through a free course with the University of Michigan that I realized that coding might have been the path I was looking for. Programming satisfied my desire to problem-solve, create, and engage with new challenges on every level. By November of the same year, I was accepted into the January 2022 Merit America Java Web Development program, and spent my time leading up to the start of the program practicing and learning Java basics. Now, less than 10 weeks shy of completing the intensive 30 week program, I'm proudly able to call myself a developer with almost a full year of hands on coding experience, and I'm ready to take on a whole new career.

September 2022 will see my completion of the Full Stack Web Application Development program, focused on hands-on coursework in Java Development, Client-Server Programming (SQL + Spring) and Frontend Development (Classic Web + React). I am now trained on software development methodologies such as Agile, 12 Factor Apps, Git and Design Patterns. In addition to conquering many individual assignments, pair programming assignments and algorithm problems, I applied my abilities by building two capstone projects featuring OOP principles, Java, Spring, Relational Databases, REST APIs and web development with React using ES6 JavaScript. (This website I built to feature my portfolio was one of my personal projects, featuring original vectors I drew myself!)

I am enthusiastic about joining an environment in the tech industry that encourages growth and professional development! Some of my personal passions and priorities include advocacy, reducing environmental impact, and protecting human rights. My dream job would be for a company that values inclusivity and diversity in their hiring practices, and allows me use my development skills in a collaborative team setting that reflects those same passions and priorities. Check out my socials or GitHub for more of my active projects, and drop me a line below if you'd like to know more!
